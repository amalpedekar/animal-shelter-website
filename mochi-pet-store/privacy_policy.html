Privacy Policy

At Mochi Pets, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers, staff, and partner shelters.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, city details, and login credentials. Staff-related data such as salary and contact information is also stored securely.

2. Use of Information

The collected data is used strictly for:

  • Managing pet adoption processes
  • Maintaining customer and staff records
  • Improving system performance and services

3. Data Protection

We ensure that all personal data is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access. Only authorized staff members can access sensitive information based on their roles.

4. User Rights

Customers have the right to:

  • Request access to their data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their personal information

5. Animal Welfare Responsibility

Mochi Pets ensures that all animals are treated ethically, provided with proper medical care, and placed in safe and suitable homes through responsible adoption processes.

6. Transparency and Accountability

We maintain clear and accurate records of vaccinations, training sessions, and adoption status. Any errors or inconsistencies are addressed promptly.

7. Cybersecurity

Our system implements security measures to protect:

  • Customer login credentials
  • Staff information
  • Adoption and medical records

8. Ethical Practices

We are committed to:

  • Using data only for legitimate purposes
  • Avoiding unnecessary data collection
  • Ensuring fairness in adoption processes
  • Maintaining honesty about pet health and behavior
