About Mochi Pets 🐾

Mochi Pets is a pet adoption platform dedicated to connecting loving families with animals in need of a home. Our goal is to create a safe, transparent, and efficient system where customers can explore pets, learn about their background, and complete the adoption process smoothly.

We work closely with shelter organisations to ensure that every animal listed on our platform is properly cared for, vaccinated, and ready for adoption. Our system maintains accurate records of pets, adoption applications, and customer information to support responsible pet ownership.

At Mochi Pets, we believe that adopting a pet is a lifelong commitment. That is why we focus on providing clear and honest information about each animal.
